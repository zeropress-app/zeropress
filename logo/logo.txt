### Why the Cube?

Most CMS platforms represent speed, security, or cloud infrastructure.

ZeroPress represents something different.

It represents the artifact.

ZeroPress does not exist to run servers.\
It exists to produce durable HTML files.

The cube symbolizes:

* Content assembled with structure

* Builds that can be reproduced

* Files that can outlive runtimes

* Publishing without operational burden

ZeroPress is not a platform.\
It is a publishing engine.

The cube reflects that idea.
